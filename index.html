Geographic overview of the resorts relative to Geneva airport. All are within 1–2.5 hours drive.

🇫🇷 Three Valleys
~2h15 from Geneva via Moûtiers. Courchevel, Méribel, Val Thorens all interconnected by lifts. One pass = 600 km.
🇨🇭 Verbier
~1h45 from Geneva. Part of Switzerland's 4 Vallées. 410 km of pistes. Famous freeride.
🇫🇷 Chamonix
~1h from Geneva. At the foot of Mont Blanc. 5 separate ski areas connected by bus/car. Real mountain town.
